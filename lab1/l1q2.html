Department of Information Technology

The Department of Information Technology offers Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT) program designed to train students in software development, networking, and modern IT solutions. Founded in 2009, the department has graduates working across top multinational firms globally.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT)
  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
  • Master of Information Technology (MIT)
  • Bachelor of Science in Computing (BSc. Computing)

Facilities

  1. State-of-the-art Networking Lab
  2. Cybersecurity Research Center
  3. 24/7 Online Library Portal
  4. Cloud Infrastructure for Student Projects
